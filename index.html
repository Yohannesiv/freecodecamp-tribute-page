Dr. Akililu Lemma

Here's a time line of DR. Akililu Lemma's life:

  • 1935 - He was born on September 18 in Jijiga, Hararghe Province, Ethiopia
  • 1959 - He graduated from Addis Ababa University College with a Bachelor of Science degree
  • 1964 - He obtained his Doctor of Science degree in Pathobiology from Johns Hopkins University in the U.S.1. He also discovered that the endod plant (Phytolacca dodecandra) can be used as a natural molluscicide to kill the snail hosts of the bilharzia parasite.
  • 1966 - He founded the Institute of Pathobiology at Addis Ababa University and became its first director.
  • 1974 - He was joined by Dr. Legesse Wolde-Yohannes, who became his research associate and collaborator on endod studies.
